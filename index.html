The PicoFlow Agentic Framework is currently available in JavaScript, chosen to align with the large and established microservices developer ecosystem. By integrating directly with NestJS, we enable Node.js developers to rapidly create production-ready Agentic endpoints with minimal friction. We expect a future Python version will be available. We also use the basic abstraction of LangChain to interface with LLMs

One of PicoFlow’s primary goals is to enable average developers—without deep AI training—to be productive within just days. The framework is designed with developer-friendliness in mind, featuring intuitive APIs and comprehensive documentation that makes it easy to get started quickly while still providing advanced capabilities for complex use cases.

Our team has extensive experience with multi-faceted RAG techniques including various chunking strategies such as overlapping, recursive, late-chunking, and summarization. We also work with re-rankers to improve retrieval quality and Graph-assisted RAG for more sophisticated knowledge representation and retrieval patterns.

our story
